An analysis of my Profile Career Keys (PCK) assessment highlights a combination of interests and abilities that are highly relevant to a career in game development. The results provide a data-driven look at my core strengths.

This is complemented by my secondary Artistic and tertiary Investigative interests. The Artistic interest aligns with creating engaging worlds, while the Investigative interest relates to complex problem-solving and debugging. A crucial convergence for game development.

This suggests a motivation to complete challenging projects, thrive in collaborative and supportive team environments, and pursue opportunities for growth. These values align well with the project-based, team-oriented nature of the game development industry.
